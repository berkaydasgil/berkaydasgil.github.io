Future plans for the blog

2021-05-29
3 minute read
Python , Data Visualization , Debugging , Deep Learning , dotNet , Leetcode , Java , C++

Plans

Some plans for the blog are:

  • Data visualizations. Would mostly be related to AI applications. I have a fleeting interest in other topics. As I am mostly working on AI those kinds of posts are more likely.

  • Deep learning related posts on image and natural language. Performance scaling, benchmarking, creating services are my area of expertise. Systems topics are also in the ballpark.

  • My experience with JAM stack and running this static Hugo blog. My future development plans on this stack.

  • I plan to write about hosting a SQL database on Github Pages to be used in static websites. This database hosted on CDN can be later used for visualizations in blog posts of static web pages. Inspired by a post1 shared on Hacker News, I realized the potential of static web pages and CDN hosting. I believe static web medium is valuable and I plan to work on further integration of these tools. I also consider working on integrating Jupyter notebooks to blog format that is suitable for static hosting. That should also be worthwhile.

  • Posts about debugging byte code languages such as .Net and Java.

  • Posts about debugging and disassembling Windows binaries.

  • Posts about Leetcode. On the site, I currently primarily use Python. From time to time pythonic way of doing things feels out of place or redundant for Leetcoding purposes. Will make the switch to Java or C++ as they would be a better fit for the task. I am currently around 50 questions mark. As currently being a novice on the platform, I expect a lot of discussion points and opportunities to write on. I have plans of creating structural notes and explaining concepts in different ways. I drafted a project where will be analyzing some stats with visualizations related to my Leetcoding performance. It is one of the plans in the making.

  • Furthermore, stuff out of my profession such as, composing music (I am recently interested in the ones that live on the web and are dynamically generated), discussions on books, and articles that I find to be interesting. And also maybe my language learning struggles. I currently mingle a little bit with Dutch and Japanese. I do it very casually and haven’t put a serious effort yet. But language concepts and learning related topics like spaced repetition are a long-time interest of mine. I will surely come to write about those in the near future.

During my education, I did a lot of academic writing and research. I want to bring those skills to this blogging platform. But this transition should take some time as I figure out my style for blog posts and pinpoint interesting topics suitable to be shared in a blog format.

I am currently planning on bringing topics that have visual aspects as they would be the ones that are most pleasant till I improve on my writing style. However, those are the ones that require a lot of preparation and would require me to get efficient at it or put some significant time in. That is definitely in the plans, but I am not certain on how and when it will take shape.

You can write to me to discuss anything regarding mentioned topics that pique your interest, or that you are experienced in and further want to reach out. Having those would be lovely. Currently, the comments for posts are closed, but please don’t hesitate to email me.
